My Commitments

01

I will not sacrifice sleep for productivity

Rest is not laziness. My best work comes from a well-rested mind, not an exhausted one.

02

I will not say yes when I mean no

People-pleasing has a cost. My time and energy deserve protection, and honesty is kinder than resentment.

03

I will not compare my journey to others

Everyone is running their own race. My progress is valid even if it looks different from someone else's.

04

I will not scroll mindlessly

My attention is valuable. I choose presence over passive consumption.

05

I will not hold onto relationships that drain me

Some connections are meant to evolve or end. Letting go is an act of self-respect.

06

I will not wait for perfect conditions

Perfection is a myth that keeps us stuck. Done is better than perfect.
