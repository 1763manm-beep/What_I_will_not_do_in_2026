What Will I Not Do in 2026

A personal declaration of boundaries, clarity, and intentional choices. This is not a to-do list — it’s a list of what I consciously leave behind.

My Commitments

01

I will never go to bed feeling sad

Sleep is closure. I choose peace over replaying the day’s weight.

02

I will not say yes to everything

Not every request deserves my agreement. Selective yeses protect my energy.

03

I will never ignore my parents’ calls

Some calls matter more than convenience. Presence is gratitude.

04

I will never apologize unnecessarily

Confidence doesn’t need permission. Respect starts with self-respect.

05

I will not confuse work with life

Work supports my life — it does not replace it.

06

I will never drag unnecessary nonsense

Clarity over chaos. I walk away from wasted energy.

07

I will never act out of peer pressure

My choices are mine alone. Alignment matters more than approval.

08

I will never open my laptop on vacation

Rest is intentional. If I’m away, I’m truly away.

09

I will never over-explain myself

Those who understand don’t need explanations.

10

I will never follow trends just to fade

Timeless values outlast temporary attention.

11

I will never hesitate to book a vacation

Memories are investments. Life isn’t meant to be postponed.

Accountability Rule

“Every time I break a rule, ₹500 goes into my piggy account.”
